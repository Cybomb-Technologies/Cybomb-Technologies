Everything you need to know about StorySpark

StorySpark is designed for children ages 2-10. Our content automatically adjusts to your child's age and reading level, with:

  • Simple interactive picture books for toddlers (ages 2-4)
  • Early reader stories with basic choices (ages 4-6)
  • Chapter-style adventures with multiple paths (ages 6-10)

Our personalization engine lets you create a profile for your child with:

  • Name and nickname that appears in stories
  • Appearance (hair color, skin tone, glasses, etc.)
  • Favorite colors, animals, and interests
  • Reading level and preferred story themes

These details are woven naturally into our stories, making your child the protagonist of their own adventures!
